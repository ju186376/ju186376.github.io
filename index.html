About Me

I am currently a junior here at UCF and working towards my degree in Digital Media Web Design. When I first started here at UCF in 2021, I was a Computer Science major and thought that it was what I always wanted to do. It was until I came up here and took a few classes that I came to realize that I had no interest in the subject matter and because of that began to do bad in those classes. It was when I was at my lowest that I was able to see that this wasn't for me and looked at other things that caught my interest. It was then that I stumbled on Digital Media Web Design and realized that I wanted to lean more towards that side of coding, a more specified side that also takes into account the creative side I cultivated in high school that seeminlgy was just for fun until recently. I am now much happier and like learning new things in order to make websites both functional and looking visually appealing.
